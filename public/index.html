Services

Full Design Service

During my visit to your home or business, we'll discuss the space you would like to redesign and I'll get a feel for the direction that you would like the design to take. If you decide to proceed, then for a fixed fee I will design the space to completion, sending you inspiration boards showing the design direction, product picks and space planning.

I love to source unique, personality inspired items for you. This design is yours and you can at this stage elect to move forward to installation. If so, we'll agree on a final design and budget. I'll begin purchasing and ordering, liasing with sub contractors from painters and electricians, to window installers, plumbers and kitchen fitters. I'll organise all deliveries to your home and oversee everything until the day of install, when I'll accessorise and style your space to completion!

I am here to relieve the stress of renovating and to bring new ideas to your household or work space.



E-Design Service

My e-design service is a different way of us working together. This option lets you achieve a redesigned space without committing to the full design service. I can come to your home for a consultation, talk with you, see your space, it's light, take pictures and measurements or you can take the pictures and measure up yourself and then email that information to me.

I then get designing to create a complete design with full product list and a detailed layout enabling you to carry out all the work yourself.



Home Staging Service

Let me prepare, package and present your property for sale. Giving your space the edge in the property market, achieving a quick sale and a higher sale price.

Price and location are what draw the buyers to your home but it is the buyers impressions when they walk through your front door that are then the most important. These feelings are formed within moments of entering your property.

The statistics are there, dressing for sale works! Please contact me for more information.
